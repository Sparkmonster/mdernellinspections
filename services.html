About Radon Testing

Radon testing is vitally important when purchasing a new home or property. Radon is a global and natural radioactive gas stemming from the breakdown of uranium in soil and rock. Nobody wants to be breathing in radioactive air. This is a dangerous health risk to owners and future owners. Getting your home professionally scanned will save you time and prevent future health and safety hazzards that may arise.


About Roof Inspection

Roof inspections are integral to maintain the stability of property. When roofs leak, this leads to moisture build up with eventual flooding and mold growth. Having them professionally inspected will ensure proper repair and prevent future disrepair. Some repairs to roofs can do more harm than good and can lead to costly re-repairs later. Roofs need to be protection rated due to potential hail/severe weather damage also.


About Thermal Scanning

Allowing for professionals to run thermal scans of your property can help detect hot and cold spots. Areas varying in temperature can determine how the ventilation is flowing and reveal potential leaks.
